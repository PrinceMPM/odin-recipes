Baked Tomato Slices

Home

Description

Baked tomato slices are a simple yet flavorful dish made by layering fresh, ripe tomato slices on a baking sheet, seasoning them with herbs and spices, and roasting them in the oven until tender and slightly caramelized. Typically, the tomatoes are sprinkled with salt, pepper, garlic, and dried or fresh herbs like basil, oregano, or thyme. Some variations include a topping of grated cheese—such as Parmesan or mozzarella—which melts and forms a delicious, golden crust. The baking process intensifies the natural sweetness of the tomatoes and brings out a savory depth that's both comforting and light.

This dish is incredibly versatile and can be served as a side, appetizer, or even a light main course when paired with crusty bread or a salad. It's particularly popular in the summer when tomatoes are in peak season and bursting with flavor. Baked tomato slices are not only easy to prepare but also healthy, low in calories, and rich in antioxidants like lycopene. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a vibrant addition to your dinner table, this dish offers a perfect balance of simplicity and taste.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large ripe tomatoes (beefsteak or heirloom work well
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder or 1 clove fresh garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Bread crumbs (optional, for extra crunch)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the tomatoes into 1/2-inch thick rounds and lay them out on a paper towel. Pat them dry to remove excess moisture.
  3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.
  4. Arrange the tomato slices in a single layer on the baking sheet.
  5. Drizzle olive oil evenly over the tomato slices.
  6. Season the slices with salt, pepper, garlic powder (or minced garlic), and dried oregano or Italian seasoning.
  7. Optional: Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and/or breadcrumbs on top of each slice for extra flavor and crunch.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15–20 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and the edges are slightly browned.
  9. Optional: Broil for 1–2 minutes at the end if you want a crispier top.
  10. Remove from oven and let cool slightly.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and serve warm.
